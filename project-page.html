My process 

Attain Information

Either through email, text, or video call, you give me any specifics/necessities you need for your store.

Design everything in Shopify/VSCode

I will then begin creating the store on Shopify/VSCode, making it look better than you can imagine.

Feedback/Changes

You review it, if there are any kinks that need to be ironed out, or any other additions you want to be added that you did not think of earlier, I will make.

Watch the customers roll in

The fun part of watching the revenue from your store pile up. Any time the store needs updating, I will come through and make them at your request.
